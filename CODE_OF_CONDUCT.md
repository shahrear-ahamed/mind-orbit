# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

At **Novoran**, we are committed to fostering a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone involved in the development of **MindOrbit**.

All contributors, employees, contractors, and collaborators are expected to maintain a culture of professionalism, collaboration, and mutual respect.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants in this project are expected to:

* Be respectful and considerate in communication
* Provide constructive feedback
* Collaborate openly and professionally
* Respect differing opinions and experiences
* Focus on improving the project and supporting teammates

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment or discrimination of any kind
* Personal attacks or insulting language
* Public or private harassment
* Sharing confidential or proprietary information without authorization
* Any conduct that is harmful to the team or organization

---

## Confidentiality

MindOrbit is a **private proprietary project owned by Novoran**.

All contributors must:

* Protect confidential company information
* Avoid sharing internal discussions outside the organization
* Follow company policies regarding intellectual property

---

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers or management team.

Reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled appropriately.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove or restrict access to contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by industry best practices used in professional software teams.
